Dog grooming appointment books and client log books are actively searched on Amazon - and most of what’s currently available is generic, low-quality, and written by people who have never set foot in a grooming salon. ✓
Low-content, high-margin, quick to produce
A dog grooming client record book - breed notes, coat type, service history, pricing, appointment slots - can be designed and live on Amazon within days. It requires no writing, just smart formatting from someone who understands the workflow.

Publish multiple variants to multiply listings
Dog grooming client log · Grooming appointment planner · Pet salon job tracker. Each targets a slightly different keyword and appears as a separate listing. A catalogue of three log books could generate £500–£1,500/mo combined with minimal ongoing effort.

Someone who buys the Blueprint to start their business will also want a client log book to run it. Amazon’s “customers also bought” algorithm will surface both titles together, compounding visibility across the catalogue.

Keyword & Advertising Strategy
Amazon Advertising Opportunity
Low competition = low cost-per-click. With fewer than 15 competing titles bidding on these keywords, even a modest Amazon Ads budget delivers strong reach. A new book in this category can move to page one faster - and more cheaply - than in almost any mainstream niche.
1
Auto campaign first - let Amazon find the buyers
Start with a Sponsored Products Auto campaign at £5–10/day for the first 4 weeks. Let Amazon identify which search terms actually convert in this niche before spending on manual bids.
2
Manual campaign on the core keywords after 4 weeks
Bid specifically on “dog grooming business book,” “how to start a dog grooming business,” and “pet grooming guide” with exact match bids. Target 30–40% ACOS in this low-competition environment.
3
Separate log book campaign once published
Target “dog grooming client record book,” “grooming appointment book,” and “pet salon log book” in a dedicated low-budget campaign. Low CPC in this sub-niche makes even £3/day effective.
